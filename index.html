Momos

Momos are a kind of dumpling popular in Nepal, Sikkim and Tibet,and are similar to Chinese potstickers. Momos are made of a simple flour and water dough. White flour is generally preferred, and sometimes a little yeast or baking soda is added to give a more 'doughy' texture to the finished product.
